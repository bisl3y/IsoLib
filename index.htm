Scroll in and out of the map using the mouse wheel. Hold and drag the mouse wheel to move around the map.

Use the 'Z' and 'X' keys to rotate the map. Press 'O' to toggle landscaping mode (click to raise ground, ALT+click to lower.)

Use number keys to select tile types. 1: Grass, 2: Paving, 3: Crate

Use the 'Q' and 'W' keys to switch render style (2d not fully implemented at present.)
